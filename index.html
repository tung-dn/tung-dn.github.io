I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Climate Finance at the University of Manchester, UK.

My main research interest is empirical studies at the intersection of finance and macroeconomics. My work covers topics including climate change and finance, economic and financial impacts of natural disasters, and uncertainty shocks and corporate policies. My broader research interests include multidisciplinary issues that are related to finance, environment, and public policy. I am also interested in using data science and econometrics to solve social issues.

I obtained a Ph.D. in Finance from the University of Surrey (UK), a M.Sc. in Financial Economics from the University of Essex (UK), and a B.Sc. in Financial Mathematics from National Economics University (Vietnam).

Email: tung.nguyen@manchester.ac.uk

Research Interests: Climate Finance, Corporate Finance, Household Finance, Public Policy, and Fintech.
